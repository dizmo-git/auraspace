Who We Are

A Space Built for Odesa's Creative Community

AuraSpace opened in 2019 on Derybasivska street with one goal in mind — to create a place where specialty coffee meets Odesa's creative culture. We are not just a cafe. We are a meeting point for artists, writers, developers and anyone who appreciates a well-crafted cup in a thoughtful space.

Our History

How It All Started

2019

Grand Opening on Derybasivska

AuraSpace opened its doors in the heart of Odesa with a small team of three baristas and a clear vision for specialty coffee culture.

2022

Best Specialty Cafe — Odesa Food Awards

Three years after opening, AuraSpace was recognised as the best specialty cafe in Odesa by the annual Odesa Food Awards jury.

2024

Creative Hub and Co-working Zone

We expanded our space to include a dedicated co-working area, hosting workshops, art exhibitions and community events.
